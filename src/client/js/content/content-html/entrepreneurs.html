Persons with Disabilities

Entrepreneurs with Disabilities Program
Manitobans who have a disability can access a network of business professionals and a world of resources through the Entrepreneurs with Disabilities Program (EDP). The EDP program provides business information, training and development, mentoring and one-on-one counseling services.

In rural communities, the program is delivered through local Community Futures offices.
